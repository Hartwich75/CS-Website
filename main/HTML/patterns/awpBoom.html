AWP Boom

The AWP Boom is a classified skin from the eSports 2013 collection, which got added to the game on the 14th August 2013. It’s a very old and popular skin, which has a lot of unique patterns. The skin itself is orange and contains different pop messages.This is “Blam!”, “Boom!”, ”Crack!”, and ”Pow!”. The most desired patterns are where the “Boom!” is centered on the skin, and where the “Crack!” is over the hole at the handle of the AWP, so it’s showing “C*ck!” instead of “Crack!”. It’s also possible to get a double “Boom!” centered.
